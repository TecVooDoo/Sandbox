## Art Style Notes

**Direction TBD:**
- HD Pixel (River City Girls, Scott Pilgrim) - Achievable solo
- Stylized 2D (Streets of Rage 4) - Animation heavy
- 2.5D/3D (TMNT Shredder's Revenge) - Technical complexity

**Visual Feedback Requirements:**
- Hit sparks - Clear impact visualization
- Screen shake - Proportional to hit power
- Freeze frames - On big hits (2-4 frames)
- Enemy flash - Damage indication
- Health bars - Floating above enemies
