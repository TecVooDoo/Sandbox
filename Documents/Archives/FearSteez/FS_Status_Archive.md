### Key Design Decisions Made

1. **2.5D Beat 'Em Up** - Side-scrolling with depth movement (not pure 2D)
2. **Day/Night Enemy Split** - Sorcerer minions by day, zombies by night
3. **RPG Elements** - XP, levels, stats inspired by Blaster Master
4. **Real Music Integration** - FearSteez's actual tracks power the game
5. **Unity 6** - Target platform for modern features
